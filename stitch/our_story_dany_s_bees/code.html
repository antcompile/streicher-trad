Our Story

Crafting Heritage Since 1980

The Beginning

Tradition Born in Barhalioun

Our journey didn't start in a factory, but in the rugged terraces of Barhalioun. In the early 1980s, the Dany family began a deep conversation with the land—one that required patience, respect, and a watchful eye on the seasons.

What began as a small family passion blossomed into a lifelong commitment to preserving the purity of Lebanese honey. Every jar we produce today carries the memory of those first golden harvests and the artisanal wisdom passed down through generations.
