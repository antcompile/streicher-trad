The Health Benefits of Raw Lebanese Honey

Dany Abi-Karam

Master Beekeeper & Founder

Deep in the rugged peaks of Mount Lebanon, where the wild thyme blooms and cedar trees stand sentinel, our bees harvest a liquid gold that is far more than just a sweetener.

Nature's Own Antibiotic

Raw honey from our high-altitude hives is never heated or micro-filtered. This preserves the naturally occurring hydrogen peroxide and bee-defensin-1, a protein that bees add to honey to help protect the hive. When you consume Dany's Bees honey, you are ingesting a potent, bio-available shield against seasonal ailments.

hexagon

Did You Know?

Lebanese Oak Honey is particularly rich in minerals and antioxidants compared to floral varieties. It is one of the few honeys that doesn't crystallize quickly due to its high mineral content and low sugar profile.

The Terroir of Lebanon

The health benefits are intrinsically linked to the biodiversity of our landscape. Our bees forage on over 200 species of wildflowers, many of which are endemic to our region. This diversity creates a complex "pollinate-profile" that supports immune health and provides a natural boost of energy that doesn't cause the typical sugar crash.

Every jar of Dany's Bees is a snapshot of a season—the rain, the wind, and the specific flowers that bloomed. It is a living product, teeming with enzymes that aid digestion and local pollens that can help build resistance to local allergens.
