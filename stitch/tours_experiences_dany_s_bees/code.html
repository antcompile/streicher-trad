The Journey Through the Hive

Arrive

Welcome to our mountain sanctuary. Start with a refreshing honey-lemon infusion and a brief overview of the apiary's history.

2

Safety first. We provide professional-grade beekeeping suits and gloves to ensure a comfortable and secure encounter.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear? expand_more
Please wear closed-toe shoes and long trousers. Avoid wearing strong perfumes or hairsprays, as scents can sometimes agitate the bees. We provide the full protective suit.
Is it safe for children? expand_more
Yes! Children aged 6 and up are welcome. We have specialized suits in smaller sizes and our beekeepers are experts at guiding young explorers.
What happens if it rains? expand_more
Bees stay inside when it rains. If the weather is unfavorable, we will contact you to reschedule or provide a full refund.
How far is the apiary from Beirut? expand_more
We are located approximately 45 minutes from Beirut by car, nestled in the serene mountains near the Chouf Cedar Reserve.
