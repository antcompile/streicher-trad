The Thyme Harvest of Mount Lebanon

Collected at altitudes exceeding 1,500 meters, our Wild Thyme honey is the essence of the Lebanese mountainside. As the summer heat peaks, the rugged slopes of Mount Lebanon become carpeted in the purple blooms of Thymus syriacus.

Our bees forage through these aromatic landscapes, concentrating the medicinal properties of the wild herbs into a viscous, amber elixir. Unlike commercial honeys, this harvest is cold-pressed and unfiltered to maintain the high pollen count and enzyme integrity that gives Wild Thyme honey its legendary status in artisanal Lebanese traditions.
